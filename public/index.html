Keqin ‘Catherine’ Ding

I am currently a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering supervised by Dr. Nitish Thakor at the Johns Hopkins University (expected graduation: 12/2025).

My research focuses on studying the integration of artificial tactile sensations for advancing neural prostheses and human-machine interfaces. My thesis involves developing computational techniques to investigate the spatial stability of sensory stimulation, and using network methods to understand cortical responses to sensory stimulation.
