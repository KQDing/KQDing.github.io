I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Biomedical Engineering supervised by Prof. Nitish Thakor at The Johns Hopkins University. My research interests are sensory perception, sensory feedback, neuroprostheses, and brain-computer interfaces. My research focuses on revealing cortical responses to sensory stimulation for upper limb prostheses and spatial stability of sensory stimulation projected fields.
